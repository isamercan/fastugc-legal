# FastUGC Support

Need help? We'd love to hear from you.

## Contact
**Email:** support@fastugc.app
**Response time:** within 48 hours

## Common questions

### How do I cancel my subscription?
iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → FastUGC → Cancel Subscription.

### How do I delete my account?
Profile tab → Delete Account. All your data is removed within 30 days.

### My video isn't generating
1. Check your internet connection
2. Make sure your script doesn't exceed the character limit
3. Force-quit and reopen FastUGC
4. If the problem persists, email us with a screenshot and the project name

### Can I use the generated videos in paid ads?
Yes — generated videos are licensed for commercial use, including paid ad campaigns, organic social posts, and client work. See Terms of Service §7 for the full license and restrictions.

### How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Paywall → "Restore Purchases" at the bottom.
